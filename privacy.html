MedAccel Legal Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

MedAccel Legal is a HIPAA-compliant platform that enables personal-injury law firms to securely retrieve and organize client medical records with patient consent via Epic’s Share Everywhere and FHIR APIs.

Data We Process

  • Protected Health Information (PHI) only with signed HIPAA authorization or patient-granted Share Everywhere access
  • Law-firm account and case metadata

Security & Compliance

All PHI is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We maintain Business Associate Agreements with covered entities and undergo regular third-party security audits.

Your Rights

Clients may revoke access or request deletion of their data at any time through their law firm.
