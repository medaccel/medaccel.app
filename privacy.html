Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

MedAccel Legal ("we," "us," or "our") operates a HIPAA-compliant platform that enables personal-injury and mass-tort law firms to securely retrieve, organize, and process client medical records. We only access Protected Health Information (PHI) when the patient (or their authorized legal representative) has provided explicit consent through a signed HIPAA authorization, patient portal consent, or other legally valid mechanism.

Information We Collect and Receive

  • Protected Health Information (PHI) solely with patient consent
  • Law-firm account details and case metadata
  • Technical and usage logs for security and audit purposes

How We Use Information

We use PHI exclusively to fulfill authorized record requests for the law firm representing the patient. We do not sell, rent, or share PHI for marketing or any unauthorized purpose.

Security & Compliance

All PHI is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We maintain Business Associate Agreements with covered entities and undergo regular third-party security audits.

Your Rights

Patients and law firms may revoke access or request deletion of data at any time by contacting us or through the MedAccel dashboard.
