Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

MedAccel Legal ("we," "us," or "our") is a HIPAA-compliant platform that enables personal-injury and mass-tort law firms to securely retrieve and organize client medical records with explicit patient consent via Epic’s Share Everywhere and FHIR APIs.

Information We Collect

  • Protected Health Information (PHI) only when the patient (or their legal representative) grants access via an 8-digit Share Everywhere code or OAuth consent
  • Law-firm account information and case metadata
  • Technical logs for security and auditing purposes

How We Use Information

We use PHI solely to retrieve, organize, and deliver medical records to the authorized law firm. We do not sell, rent, or share PHI for marketing or any non-authorized purpose.

Security & HIPAA Compliance

All PHI is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We maintain active Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with covered entities and undergo regular third-party security audits (SOC 2, HITRUST in progress).

Your Rights

Patients may revoke access or request deletion of their data at any time by contacting their law firm or us directly. Law firms may also delete data through their MedAccel dashboard.
